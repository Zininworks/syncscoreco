SyncScore

Classical Music with Flowing Score

High Quality Audio

We are tired of low quality, noisy recordings on App Store, too. All SyncScore albums are hand-picked with great care, and re-encoded with noise reduction if necessary. Rubinstein, Heifetz, Kempff, Böhm and so many great artists. A legendary recording never gets old.

Flowing Scores

Every song in SyncScore is accompanied with sheet music, automatically scrolled line by line. And Horizontal Progress Indicator indicates… well, horizontal progress in the line. Never lost your position while listening.

AirPrint

SyncScore also supports AirPrint. Scores are look great on your iPad with paging navigation and pinch-to-zoom feature, but you can easily print out sheet music on a sheet of dead trees if you want to. Practice a difficult piece, and turn it into your masterpiece.

Playback Rate Control

Mere mortal like us often finds a virtuoso's performance is too fast to track and analyze. With 0.5X button, You can slow down playing rate to half speed. Keep every note counts.
